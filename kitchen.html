Kitchen

The farmhouse kitchen is on the ground floor, looking out over the front garden. It’s equipped with a large farmhouse table, settee, rocking chair and wood-burning range cooker, as well as a conventional electric cooker, fridge with ice box, and washing machine.

The cupboards and shelves are kept stocked with basic food items, and all the crockery, cutlery and utensils you could wish for.

We like the kitchen! It’s become a great place to gather, cook and linger over a meal, or glass of wine.

From the kitchen, wooden stairs lead up to the rear, first floor entrance hall, off which is the bathroom, main bedroom and the living room.
