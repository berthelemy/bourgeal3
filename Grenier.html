Grenier

The grenier, accessed by stairs from the first floor entrance hall, was the old grain store for the farmhouse. It now forms two spacious single bedrooms, each with exposed beams, whitewashed stone walls and velux windows.

Electric wall heaters give a cosy background warmth, but there are also plenty of blankets and throws should you need them.
