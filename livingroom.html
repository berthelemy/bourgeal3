Living Room

The first floor living room is an inviting space, with its original beams, huge granite fireplace and wood-burning stove, and comfortable seating.

You'll find this a welcoming space to relax or to enjoy the selection of board games, books and maps at the end of a busy day.

There's an ample supply of logs and kindling in the barn to help keep you cosy on chilly nights.
