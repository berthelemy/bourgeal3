Outside

The farmhouse is located in the hamlet of Bourgeal, just 3 kilometres from the beautiful market town of Le Faouet.

Surrounded by fields and woodlands, you can take a walk down quiet country lanes straight from the back door, with barely a vehicle to disturb you.

Depending on the season, why not forage for blackberries, hazelnuts or sweet chestnuts along the way?
