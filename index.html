Arranged over three floors, the farmhouse offers accommodation for up to 8 people, with a mix of old French charm and modern facilities.

Dating from the mid-18th Century, the house retains many original features, and is set in a small hamlet with views over the open countryside.

The farmhouse kitchen on the ground floor is accessed from the front of the house.

The living room, bathroom and master bedroom are all on the first floor. Access is either from an internal wooden staircase situated in the kitchen, or from an external flight of stone steps at the rear of the house. Further sleeping accommodation is found in the second floor grenier.
